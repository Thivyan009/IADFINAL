Our Story

From Farm to Your Table

Organo was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between local farmers and health-conscious consumers in Batticaloa and beyond. Founded in 2020, we started as a small family business with a passion for organic farming and sustainable agriculture.

Our journey began when we noticed that many people in our community wanted access to fresh, organic produce but couldn't find reliable sources. Local farmers, on the other hand, had difficulty reaching customers directly. Organo became the solution - connecting the best local farms with families who value quality and freshness.

Today, we're proud to work with over 50 local farmers across the Eastern Province, ensuring that every product we deliver meets our strict standards for organic certification and quality.
